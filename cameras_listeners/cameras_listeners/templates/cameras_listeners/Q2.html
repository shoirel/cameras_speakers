What is this project about?

As you know, thousands of online meetings take place at the Commission every week. Turning the cameras on may improve communication and participants’ experience but the opposite may be true in some cases and for some people. Moreover, video streaming has some economic and environmental costs and may worsen the quality of the audio. In this project, we have several talks, delivered with vs. without the cameras, to compare everyone’s experience, holding other factors unchanged.

If you would like to share your thoughts about the pros and cons of using cameras in online meetings at the EC, please use the space below.
